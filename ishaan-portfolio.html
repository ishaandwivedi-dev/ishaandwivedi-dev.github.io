About Me

Engineering tomorrow's solutions, today.

My passion for engineering started early — assembling LEGO sets, helping put together IKEA furniture, seeing every task as a puzzle waiting to be solved. This curiosity led me to join my school's robotics team in fourth grade, and I've been building ever since.

Currently, I'm a third-year Mechanical Engineering student at Northeastern, where I've sharpened my skills through hands-on co-op experiences at Rheem Manufacturing and SharkNinja. I specialize in product design, CAD modeling, and bringing concepts from sketch to prototype.
